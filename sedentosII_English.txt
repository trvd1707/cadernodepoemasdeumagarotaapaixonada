                        THIRSTY FOR LOVE II<br><br>
                      </FONT>
                      <FONT class=poemText face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">
                        <FONT class=poemSubtitle face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">(a fugue)</FONT><br>

                        How sad the life of lovers is!<br>
                        They have no past, no future.<br>
                        They make no plans<br>
                        and live on crumbs,<br>
                        in fits and starts.<br>
                        They have neither themselves,<br>
                        nor the other.<br>
                        They only have a desire and a love<br>
                        that evaporates<br>
                        <font size=4>and fades away...</font>
                        <font size=3>away...</font>
                        <font size=2>away...</font>
