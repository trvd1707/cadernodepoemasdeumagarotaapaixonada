                      <FONT class=poemTitle face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">
                        Pink Trumpet Tree </FONT>
                        <FONT class=poemText face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif"><br><br>
The tip of a branch from the pink trumpet tree<br>
That keeps peeking at me<br>
In the window corner.<br>
<br>
When it is in bloom<br>
I smile.<br>
When it is full of leaves<br>
I think.<br>
When it is dry<br>
I feel such great anguish!<br>
<br>
Such is our vanity.<br>
