                      <FONT class=poemTitle face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">
                        ROOM WITH A VIEW<br><br>
                      </FONT>
                      <FONT class=poemDedication face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">To Oleg and Tamara Greshner</FONT><br>
                      <FONT class=poemText face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif"><br>
                        In front of my window<br>
                        There is a rose bush of white roses.<br>
                        As I look out,<br>
                        The white roses simply exist,<br>
                        Swaying gently.<br>
                        <br>
                        I smile at the white roses<br>
                        Looking out the window.<br>
                        Whose window is it,<br>
                        Mine or theirs?<br></p>
                      </FONT>
