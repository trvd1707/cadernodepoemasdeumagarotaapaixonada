                        Luminosity
                        <br><br>
                      </FONT>
                      <FONT class=poemText face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">

                        If it is day<br>
                        The light shines<br>
                        The heart smiles<br>
                        <br>
                        If it is night<br>
                        I shine the light<br>
                        The heart smiles<br>
                        <br>
                        The light that shines<br>
                        Is a consequence<br>
                        Of the smile</p>
