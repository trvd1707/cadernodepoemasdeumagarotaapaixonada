                        CHARACTERS<br><br>
                      </FONT>
                      <FONT class=poemText face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">
                        We are characters<br>
                        That we ourselves created.<br>
                        Who existed?<br>
                        Who was I?<br>
                        <br>
                        And I play my role.<br>
                        I live my life<br>
                        As if it were someone else's.<br>
                        As if it were not mine.
                        </p>
